FlexBox Tutorial

What is Flexbox

A flexbox is a lay a collection of items out in one direction or another.

Types of Flexbox

The flex container Types are:

  • Flex Direction
  • Flex Flow
  • Align Item
  • Align Content
  • Flex Wrap

The flex Direction Property

It can have one of four Properties: row , row-reverse , column , or column-reverse
